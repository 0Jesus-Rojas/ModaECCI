Moda ECCI

What is the purpose of this page?

On this page I will talk about the experience lived and the concepts and theories obtained in the Moda ECCI event, a space created for the visualization of student projects, design, creativity applied to the fashion environment, where I learned mainly color theory.

Objective

To tell my experience in the event and summarize everything learned so that anyone who wants to know how the event was and what was learned there, can review my page to know how it was and learn what was told in that place.
